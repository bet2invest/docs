# Welcome

Bet2Invest is a platform designed to bring clarity, reliability, automation, and advanced analytics to sports betting. Here, you can explore [certified experts](https://bet2invest.com/tipsters) statistics, understand real long-term performance, and follow betting strategies backed by data rather than guesswork. Every tip published on Bet2Invest is tracked and verified using **official Pinnacle real-time odds**, ensuring full transparency and eliminating the possibility of manipulated results.

***

You can use Bet2Invest in different ways depending on your experience and what you are looking for.&#x20;

* Beginners can discover proven [experts](/broken/pages/I6Y4fEAry6mV7ep17hiO) and learn how to analyse betting performance.
* Experienced bettors can deepen their strategy through advanced metrics (CLEV, CLEV, Liquidity...) and market analysis.
* Tipsters can eventually monetize their expertise by sharing their predictions once they meet the [requirements](experts/pro-requirements.md) and are manually verified by Bet2Invest.
